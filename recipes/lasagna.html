Lasagna Recipe!



This is a photo of my lasagna

Ingredients List:


  • Pasta Sauce
  • Ricotta
  • Shells
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. Lay out shells in a sprayed pain
  3. Place a layer of ricotta
  4. Place browned beef and cover with sauce
  5. Repeat until out of supplies or at the top of the pan
